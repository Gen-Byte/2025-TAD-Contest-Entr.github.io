Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial intelligence is a computer program capable of thinking and doing task similar to that of humans, the only difference is that Artificial Intelligence can do it better than the human can.

The Model



An AI learns from its Learning model. A learning Model uses one or more algorithms to find patterns in a given data set. Once it finds patterns in a data set, it will then train the AI to find those patterns. After the training is done, the AI will then be able to, by itself, find certain patterns in data that you give it and make predictions based on the data.

NOTE:
AI has advanced rapidly and is being implemented into multiple different fields of work. Which is why it
is important for people to know that AI is capable of discriminating between multiple factors. (ex: race, gender age, etc)

The algorithm



An algorithm is a set of instructions that run and are used for certain tasks


There are countless of algorithms for AI and each one has its own advantages and disadvatantages


The size and type of data can all affect your algorithm

Data



The basis in which our AI will stand on is data, which is why having the correct data is vital


Define what you want your AI to do then gather relevant data relating to the problem
